About Us

Vanier Veterinary Clinic has been taking care of pets for the Vanier area since 1998. We are passionate about supporting owners of all sorts of animals. We specialize in catering to dogs and cats but can provide services for many other pets or animals. We provide a warm and loving atmosphere for your pet to ensure they are never scared while in our care.

Our Location

Our clinic is located near the Rideau River in Vanier. Our current building was constructed in 2016 and features all the amenities needed in a modern clinic. Plus, our proximity to the Rideau River and trails makes it a great location to walk dogs.
